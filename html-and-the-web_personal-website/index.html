About me

I am a Berlin-based UX Engineer with a background in design and visual arts. During my career as a designer and technologist, I worked in award-winning design agencies, international non-profits and innovative Startups across Europe.

I have a passion for data visualization and enjoy working on data-heavy applications and dashboard-based products. I approach my work with a high bar for quality and craft, always prioritizing accessibility and embracing my responsibility to society and the environment.
